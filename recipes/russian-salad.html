Russian Salad

Description of Olivier

There are plenty of Pros and Cons to Olivier. While making a food dish, salads are usually regarded as the easiest dishes to make, Olivier is no exception to this. However olivier is a recipe that takes many ingredients and because of this is usually make at bigger events in bulk.

Olivier has a tangy and maybe even sour flavor depending on how you make it. It is creamy with the perfect mix of veggies and meat, both crunchy and not. Although as stated earlier it is usually made in bulk. Now lets move on the the making portion of this recipe, first you will need...

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • carrots
  • pickles
  • boiled eggs
  • sweet peas
  • onions
  • bologna/ham
  • mayonnaise
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the Carrots, Potatoes, and Eggs: In a large pot boil potatoes, and carrots. Potatoes and carrots should be boiled until easily pierced with a fork. Next, boil the eggs. Let ingredients cool.
  2. Dice salad ingredients: Dice the onions, carrots , potatoes, eggs, and bologna/ham into small cubes. Mix all ingredients that you have into a bowl until well combined.
  3. Add Dressing: Toss the salad ingredients with mayo and season with salt and pepper until satisfied. Remember this is a recipe that you control, you can have as much or as little of an ingredient as you want. This recipe is really up to the person making it.
