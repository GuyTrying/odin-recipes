Scrambled eggs

Description of Scrambled Eggs

Scrambled eggs are a really simple recipe for eggs. But because of this really simple recipe they require time to perfect. The difference in chefs makes a huge difference. So while this recipe may look simple it'll take time to master the Perfect Eggs.

Incase you have never heard of scrambled eggs witch I dought is the case, But it is a was of cooking eggs. The eggs are scrambled and have a perfect contradiction of fluffy/firm and wet/dry. They are debatably the best way to eat eggs in the morning. Now moving on the the ingredients that you will need.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup half-n-half
  • 1/4 teaspoon Kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • black pepper
  • fresh chopped herb
    •

Steps

  1. Mix Eggs: In a bowl, whisk together eggs , half-n-half, and salt until mixture is the same color and texture. It must be light and foamy, without any separation between the yolk/whites.
  2. Prepare the Pan: Melt butter into a small nonstick pan over medium heat, wait for butter to coat the whole pan with a little foam.
  3. Adding eggs: Add the eggs center of the pan, immediately reducing the heat to medium low.
  4. First half of cooking: Wait for the eggs to start setting, then push the eggs from one end of the pan to another. Continue this process pausing in between swipes to allow egg to cook. It should start forming curds.
  5. When the eggs are almost cooked: Slowly told the eggs into itself a few times, bringing them together.
  6. Take eggs of: Remove the eggs from the heat, they should still have some moisture on them .
  7. Transfer to serving plates: Put the eggs on your plate and top with some pepper adn herbs.
  8. Enjoy!
