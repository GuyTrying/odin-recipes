cookie dough

Description

3 step

Ingredients

  • unsalted butter
  • granulated sugar
  • flour

Steps

  • Make the dough:

    beat together butter, sugar, and flour until blended.
    • >

  • Form the cookies:

    form the dough into 1-inch balls. Roll the balls in sugar, then flatten them with a glass or measuring cup.

  • Bake:

    bake until just barely golden around the edges and bottom.

  • Rest:

    let rest and cool for at least 10-15 minutes before eating.
